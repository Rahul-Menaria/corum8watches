Grand Central Tourbillon


Grand Central Tourbillon

The Grand Central Tourbillon is a spectacular new collection, where the Tourbillon is in the spotlight, placed at the center of the timepiece. One of the complexities of the collection, lies in the fact that we had to totally rethink the watch in order to move the Tourbillon from its original position at 6 o’clock to the center of the watch. In addition, Corum8 designers and watchmakers had to find an innovative way to place the hour and minute hands around the Tourbillon cage, highlighting the beauty of the piece.

Vanguard Skeleton Swiss Limited Edition


Vanguard Skeleton Swiss Limited Edition

The Vanguard™ Skeleton Swiss Limited Edition, enriches the collection with a new striking red skeleton body. Paying homage to Switzerland, the colors that adorn this collection recall the pure white and intense red of the Swiss flag. Made of titanium, the case is coated with white enamel while the titanium movement is anodized in red. This limited edition of only 50 timepieces provides a mesmerizing view on the movement that is a mechanical treasure entirely crafted in-house.
